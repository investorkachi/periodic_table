How I play football

In football, I play the role of a defender. And as a defender, my role is crucial in making sure no ball gets pass me into my goal area. I focus on predicting the movements of my oponents, reading their body language and intercepting passes among them. I'm quick to react, using my agility to win the ball and pass back to my teammates. Even when under pressure, I keep my calm and stay composed, keeping my fellow defenders and teammates in shape. I use my experience to make decisive tackles and clearances when marking my opponents. I strive to maintain a good spirit of sportmanship by playing fair and carefully and helping those that i have mistakenly fouled.I take pride in being a defender on which my teammates can rely on while helping to secure victory in each season i play.
